2. **Money pages** — T2 product pages, T3 funnel pages, T5 trust pages, T4 guide profiles: one-off builds on the new design system.

URL rule: keep every slug exactly as-is (posts live at the root, e.g. `/whole-life-insurance/`, not under `/blog/`). Zero redirects needed if slugs never change.
